About This Form

This form collects various health-related information to assess the likelihood of heart disease. The factors considered include:

  • Age
  • Sex
  • Chest pain type
  • Cholesterol levels
  • Blood sugar
  • Maximum heart rate
  • Exercise-induced angina
  • Oldpeak (ST depression)
  • ST slope

Each of these factors plays a role in determining the potential risk of heart disease.

Please answer all questions accurately to get the most reliable assessment.

Important Note: This is a preliminary assessment tool and should not be considered as a definitive diagnosis. Always consult with a healthcare professional for accurate medical advice.
